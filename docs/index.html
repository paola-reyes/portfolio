Hey, there! I'm Paola.

I'm a computer science student at the University of Illinois Chicago, where I'm honing my skills in programming, software development, and UX design. I'm passionate about creating user-friendly applications that will improve people's daily lives. Whether I'm developing interactive apps or designing intuitive interfaces, I’m driven by curiosity and a desire to build meaningful solutions.

I love diving into new tech and experimenting with ideas that blend with creativity.
