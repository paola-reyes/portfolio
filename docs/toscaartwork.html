toscaartwork

Sharing a poignant commentary on identity and agency.

Overview

toscaartwork is a professional website for Chicago-based artist, Kaelyn Andrade. It is a living portfolio of her life's work and builds upon her connection to the arts community.

Functionality

An electronic gallery delving into the intersections of history and identity.

Andrade's art provides a poignant commentary on the ways history and colonization continue to influence and restrict marginalized groups, offering a unique perspective on the impact of historical and contemporary issues on identity and agency. The website presents her paintings series, among them Memorias de cobre , Amor y brujería entre generaciones , and Quinceañera Series .

Delivering art experiences to communities in Chicago.

Andrade hosts art workshops to foster the artistic growth and development of artists of all backgrounds. They serve as a place where artists can come together to expand their creativity and hone their artistic abilities. Those looking to schedule a workshop can fill out a request form on the website.
