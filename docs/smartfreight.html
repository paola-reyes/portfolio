SmartFreight Solutions

Improving cargo monitoring for secure freight shipment delivery.

Overview

SmartFreight Solutions provides live updates on freight shipment container conditions and road conditions en route to its destination.

Functionality

Meeting the unique requirements of different types of shipments.

This application is a smart way to monitor and generate reports on freight container conditions. It is designed for drivers and container owners to see live updates of their shipment. It takes into account the varied physical threshold requirements of different types of products.

Reporting sensor readings on current freight container conditions.

Our application is designed to work alongside a sensor that is placed inside the freight container. This will record temperature and g-force conditions while reporting on any potentially damaging conditions for the packages. The incident report will show events like temperature spikes and excessive freight carrier vibrations recorded throughout the trip.

Providing optimized route suggestions for freight carrier safety.

The route optimization feature takes into account recent weather conditions and traffic indicents to provide the safest route towards the destination. The shipment map will display the current best route alongside the carrier's location.
