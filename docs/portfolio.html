Python

Summary

This project contributed to research for The Urban Toolkit (UTK), an open-source 3D visualization framework that for spatial-temporal urban data visualization. Our team aimed to explore how to efficiently visualize spatial-temporal data in a 3D environment. We approached this by incorporating the effective thematic data plotting tools, like VegaLite, to supplement UTK's spatial visualization.

Summary

This is a personal website to showcase toscaartwork's portfolio of art pieces. The website features a gallery of artwork and contact information for potential clients or collaborators. In her art, she addresses the societal restrictions imposed on women and girls in both Mexico and El Salvador. She uses visual imagery from social media—often functioning as modern political cartoons—to critique how history and colonization have shaped societal expectations and limitations.

Summary

This web app aims to enhance global shipping logistics by improving cargo routing and monitoring to meet the unique requirements of every shipment.
