BookClub

A platform for book enthusiasts to explore and read the classics.

Overview

BookClub is a web application designed for book lovers to discover and read classic literature. The platform offers a curated selection of timeless books along with features that enhance our user's e-reading experience.

Functionality

Choose among hundreds of public domain ebooks to read online.

The main Functionality of this app allows you to choose a book from the collection and read it in our online reader. This app uses Project Gutenberg's Gutendex API to fetch from its extensive library of free ebooks and organized book classification system. The ebook is built using Readium API to make the app EPUB-compliant.

Read ebooks using our personalized e-reader.

The e-reader includes personalization features such as single-page or double-page view, adjustable font size and font-family, and background color options such as light mode, dark mode, and café mode. It also tracks your progress with a reading progress bar and saves your current page and preferences for future reading sessions.

Browse a curated library of classic literature.

You can browse through the collection by genres like Popular Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy, Drama, and Romance. It also includes a search option that allows you to search for specific titles or authors.

Maintain a personal library of your favorite books.

You can create an account to save your favorite books to your personal library. This allows you to easily access and manage your collection of preferred reads.
