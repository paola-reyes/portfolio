BetterBite

Overview

BetterBite is a nutritional habit tracker that provides nutrition insights and personalized recipe recommendations. It is designed to help users make informed dietary choices and achieve their health goals.

Functionality

Starting new habits with meals you will enjoy.

BetterBite is a conscientious food-positivity platform designed to ease nutrition-related challenges. Using an initial profile quiz upon sign-up, we build your profile based on your current diet, allergies, and favorite cuisines. This allows for recipe suggestions that are tailored to your palate. This application uses Spoonacular API to reference their extensive library of nutrition and recipe information.

Connecting with our audience to meet their needs.

This project was designed based on user experience research we performed via user interviews to collect their feedback throughout the design and prototype process. We gathered insights on their values and suggestions for app design improvements.

Supporting users throughout their nutrition journey.

Our application meets users wherever they are on their health journey by fostering nutrition education and self-discovery. You can track your meals and view a summary of your nutrition habits throughout the day.

Generating quick, easy-to-understand nutriton breakdowns and summaries.

Our nutrition analysis feature generates a daily nutrition breakdown based on nutrient daily values and the foods you track that day. It reports how much of your daily value you have consumed so far for categories like calories, fiber, vitamins, protein, and iron. We also provide a summarized interpretaion of your results that tells you which nutrient categories you can focus on for the remainder of the day.

Providing personalized recipe recommendations based on your needs.

This application suggests a list of recipes based on a users's dietary preferences and the nutrition report's identified areas for improvement. We offer a variety of regional recipes so users can experience the taste of home from their own kitchen. We also support our user's desires to save money by offering several quick home-cooked meals.
